### 双重国籍 / Dual Nationals
德国队本届阵容的多元背景反映了现代德国的社会现实：穆夏拉在英格兰长大（母亲德国人、父亲尼日利亚人），曾代表英格兰U21后选择德国；马利克·蒂奥拥有塞内加尔和芬兰血统；勒鲁瓦·萨内的父亲为塞内加尔人；德尼兹·温达夫是土耳其-库尔德裔；费利克斯·恩梅查的父系为尼日利亚；阿桑·韦德拉奥果的父亲来自布基纳法索。最引人注目的是，维尔茨转会利物浦成为德国足球史上最昂贵的转会之一。纳格尔斯曼打造的"新德国"在坚持技术足球的同时，融入了更多元的文化基因。

Germany's squad reflects the diversity of modern German society: Musiala grew up in England (German mother, Nigerian father) and represented England U21 before choosing Germany; Thiaw has Senegalese and Finnish heritage; Sane's father is Senegalese; Undav is Turkish-Kurdish; Nmecha has Nigerian parentage; Ouedraogo's father is Burkinabe. Most notably, Wirtz's move to Liverpool ranks among the most expensive transfers in German football history. Nagelsmann's "New Germany" blends technical football with a multicultural DNA.

### 历史看点 / Historical Note
德国队以四届世界冠军的身份来到2026年世界杯，但背负着2018年和2022年连续两届小组出局的沉重包袱——这是德国足球史上最大的耻辱之一。纳格尔斯曼自2023年9月接手后，对球队进行了彻底的年轻化改造，维尔茨和穆夏拉成为进攻双核。40岁的诺伊尔从退役状态回归国家队，目标是在第五届世界杯上弥补生涯遗憾。德国队拥有世界级中轴线（基米希-穆夏拉/维尔茨-哈弗茨），但中锋位置仍是老问题。小组赛对阵库拉索和厄瓜多尔不容轻敌——2018年输给韩国的教训历历在目。

Germany arrives at the 2026 World Cup as four-time champions — but burdened by back-to-back group-stage exits in 2018 and 2022, among the greatest humiliations in German football history. Nagelsmann, appointed September 2023, has overseen a thorough generational refresh, with Wirtz and Musiala forming a devastating creative axis. At 40, Neuer came out of retirement for a 5th World Cup, seeking to close his international career on a redemptive note. Germany boasts a world-class spine (Kimmich–Musiala/Wirtz–Havertz), but the center-forward question persists. Group-stage matches against Curacao and Ecuador demand full respect — the 2018 Korea loss still haunts.

### 双重国籍 / Dual Nationals
库拉索队的身份故事是本届世界杯最独特的。全队26人中，**仅塔希斯·钟一人出生于库拉索本土**——其余25人全部出生在荷兰或欧洲其他国家。这些球员几乎都是库拉索裔荷兰移民的后代，通过血缘获得代表库拉索的资格。前荷兰国家队主帅艾德沃卡特（78岁，世界杯历史上最年长教练）执掌这支球队本身就是最好的注脚：一位荷兰人带领一批荷兰出生的球员为一个人口15万的加勒比海岛而战。库拉索是荷兰王国的构成国，球员们大多在家乡有祖父母或曾祖父母的关系。这支"荷兰B队"的浪漫之处在于，他们让一个面积仅444平方公里的岛屿登上了世界足球的最高舞台。

Curaçao's identity story is the most unique at this World Cup. Of the 26-man squad, **only Tahith Chong was born on the island** — the other 25 were all born in the Netherlands or elsewhere in Europe. These players are almost all descendants of Curaçaoan immigrants to the Netherlands, qualifying through ancestry. Former Netherlands national team coach Advocaat (78, the oldest coach in World Cup history) managing this team is itself the perfect metaphor: a Dutchman leading Dutch-born players fighting for a Caribbean island of 150,000 people. Curaçao is a constituent country of the Kingdom of the Netherlands — most players have grandparents or great-grandparents on the island. The romance of this "Netherlands B-team" is that it has brought a 444-square-kilometer island to world football's biggest stage.

### 历史看点 / Historical Note
库拉索是本届世界杯的童话故事。人口约15万——比一个标准足球场满座观众还少——这个加勒比小岛成为世界杯历史上最小的参赛国。他们在中北美及加勒比海地区预选赛中以7胜3平的不败战绩突围，震惊了整个足球世界。78岁的艾德沃卡特最初在晋级后宣布辞职，但在球员们集体请求下回归——这一幕成为世界杯预选赛最动人的时刻之一。库拉索的核心球员大多在荷兰联赛效力，球风兼具荷兰足球的技术性和加勒比特有的自由精神。无论小组赛结果如何，库拉索已经书写了历史。面对德国、科特迪瓦和厄瓜多尔，每一分钟都是这个岛国足球的最高光时刻。

Curaçao is the fairy tale of the 2026 World Cup. With a population of roughly 150,000 — smaller than a full stadium crowd — this Caribbean island is the smallest nation ever to qualify. They stormed through CONCACAF qualifying unbeaten (7W 3D), shocking the football world. Advocaat, 78, briefly resigned after qualification, only to return at the players' collective plea — one of the most touching moments of the qualifying cycle. Most core players compete in Dutch leagues, blending Dutch technical football with Caribbean flair and freedom. Whatever the group-stage results, Curaçao has already made history. Against Germany, Ivory Coast, and Ecuador, every minute is the island's greatest football moment.

### 双重国籍 / Dual Nationals
科特迪瓦近年来在"抢夺"双重国籍人才方面极为积极，成果显著。埃文·恩迪卡（法国出生，前法国U21）、阿尔邦·拉丰（布基纳法索出生，前法国U20）、塞科·福法纳（法国出生）、尼古拉·佩佩（法国出生）均代表法国青年队出战过后转投科特迪瓦。最具战略意义的是2026年3月的两笔"闪电归化"：埃利·瓦希和安热-约安·博尼双双从法国U21转投大象军团。不过，长期核心维尔弗里德·扎哈和2023年非洲杯冠军功臣塞巴斯蒂安·阿莱均落选大名单，法埃教练的选择引发了一些讨论。

Ivory Coast has aggressively recruited dual-national talent in recent years with striking success. Evan Ndicka (French-born, ex-France U21), Alban Lafont (Burkina Faso-born, ex-France U20), Seko Fofana (French-born), and Nicolas Pepe (French-born) all represented France at youth level before switching. The most strategic moves came in March 2026: Elye Wahi and Ange-Yoan Bonny both jumped from France U21 to the Elephants. However, longtime stalwart Wilfried Zaha and 2023 AFCON-winning hero Sebastien Haller were both left out — coach Faé's selections have generated some debate.

Ivory Coast enters their 4th World Cup, having never advanced past the group stage in three previous attempts (2006, 2010, 2014). Their crowning achievement came as 2023 AFCON champions on home soil — knocking out Senegal and Nigeria in the knockout rounds, with Faé taking over mid-tournament and lifting the trophy in a storybook run. This squad is rich in attacking talent: Amad Diallo had a breakout season at Manchester United, Wahi and Bonny are the latest products of the French academy pipeline, and Adingra boasts Champions League experience. Defensively, Ndicka and Singo form a physically imposing center-back pairing. Kessie's midfield leadership and long-range shooting are an underrated weapon. Breaking the group-stage curse is the Elephants' historic mission.

Ecuador produced a defensive miracle in CONMEBOL qualifying — conceding only 5 goals in 18 matches, the best defensive record in South America. At 36, Enner Valencia plays his 3rd World Cup — his 6 World Cup goals (3 in 2022, 3 in 2014) are the most by any Ecuadorian. Willian Pacho has ascended to world-class status after winning the Champions League with PSG, while Hincapie has flourished since joining Arsenal. The 19-year-old prodigy Kendry Paez could be one of the tournament's youngest breakout stars. Ecuador's ambition extends beyond merely matching their 2006 R16 — this defensive juggernaut has the foundation to go further.
