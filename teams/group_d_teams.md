### 双重国籍 / Dual Nationals
美国队本届世界杯阵容拥有 **13 名双重国籍球员**，为队史之最。核心包括：队长普利西奇持有克罗地亚护照（祖父移民）；巴洛贡生于纽约但父母为尼日利亚人，曾代表英格兰U21后转投美国；尤努斯·穆萨生于纽约、父母加纳人，在意大利和英格兰长大；塞尔吉诺·德斯特生于荷兰、父亲为苏里南裔美国人；马利克·蒂尔曼生于德国巴伐利亚、母亲为德国人。这种多元背景已成为美国足球的独特竞争力。

The USMNT features **13 dual-national players** in this World Cup squad, the most in program history. Key examples: captain Pulisic holds a Croatian passport via his grandfather; Balogun (born NYC to Nigerian parents) formerly represented England U21; Yunus Musah (NYC-born, Ghanaian parents, raised in Italy & England); Sergino Dest (born Netherlands to Surinamese-American father); Malik Tillman (born Bavaria to German mother). This multicultural fabric has become a defining competitive advantage.

The US opened with a commanding 4-1 win over Paraguay — Balogun brace, Pulisic two assists. Under Pochettino the team has transformed from a counter-attacking side to a high-pressing, possession-based outfit. Their best finish since the inaugural 1930 third-place was the 2002 quarterfinal. With 14 players in Europe's top-5 leagues and the deepest bench in program history, breaking the quarterfinal ceiling is the explicit goal. Pulisic, at his peak age 27, carries the armband into his first World Cup as captain.

### 双重国籍 / Dual Nationals
巴拉圭阵容以本土和南美联赛球员为主，双重国籍球员极少。唯一的例外是中场卡库·罗梅罗·加马拉，他出生于阿根廷，早年代表阿根廷青年队出战，后在成年阶段转投巴拉圭（母亲为巴拉圭人）。整体而言，这支巴拉圭队是一支高度本土化的队伍。

Paraguay's squad is overwhelmingly homegrown and South America-based, with very few dual nationals. This is a deeply localized squad rooted in the Guarani football tradition.

### 历史看点 / Historical Note
巴拉圭时隔16年重返世界杯舞台。上一次亮相还要追溯到2010年南非世界杯，当时他们历史性闯入八强（点球大战负于西班牙）。这支南美劲旅以防守硬朗著称，阿尔法罗教练打造的4-4-2体系强调中后场绞杀和快速转换。22岁的恩西索是球队绝对的技术核心，他在布莱顿和斯特拉斯堡的表现为他赢得了"新圣克鲁斯"的美誉。首战1-4不敌美国暴露了防线漏洞，但巴拉圭曾在南美预选赛中逼平巴西和阿根廷，证明他们有能力给强队制造麻烦。

Paraguay returns to the World Cup after a 16-year absence — their last appearance was South Africa 2010, where they reached a historic quarterfinal (lost on penalties to eventual champions Spain). Known for defensive resilience, coach Alfaro's 4-4-2 emphasizes midfield disruption and quick transitions. At 22, Julio Enciso is the team's crown jewel — his performances at Brighton and Strasbourg have earned him the "new Roque Santa Cruz" label. The opening 1-4 loss to the US exposed defensive frailties, but Paraguay drew Brazil and Argentina in CONMEBOL qualifying — they can trouble any opponent.

### 双重国籍 / Dual Nationals
澳大利亚国家队以其移民背景的多元性著称。本届阵容中的双重国籍故事尤为丰富：赫鲁斯蒂奇出生于波黑，内战期间随家人逃难至澳大利亚；伊兰昆达出生于坦桑尼亚难民营，父母为布隆迪难民，2岁时随家人移民澳大利亚；穆罕默德·图雷出生于几内亚，幼年移居澳洲；沃尔帕托出生于悉尼但少年时期在意大利度过，曾代表意大利U21，后被波波维奇说服转投澳大利亚——这一"抢人"成功被认为是澳大利亚足球的重大外交胜利。

The Socceroos' immigrant-background diversity is legendary. This squad's dual-national stories are especially rich: Hrustic was born in Bosnia and fled the civil war as a refugee; Irankunda was born in a Tanzanian refugee camp to Burundian parents and arrived in Australia at age two; Mohamed Toure was Guinea-born; and Cristian Volpato — raised in Italy, capped by Italy U21, then convinced by Popovic to switch to Australia — represents a major recruiting coup hailed as a diplomatic triumph for Australian football.

### 历史看点 / Historical Note
澳大利亚连续第6次参加世界杯，但本届是近20年来最年轻、最缺乏经验的阵容——17名球员首次登上世界杯舞台。队长瑞安以104次出场成为队史第二多出场的门将，35岁的莱基则迎来了个人第四届世界杯。20岁的伊兰昆达是本届世界杯最具故事性的球员之一，从坦桑尼亚难民营到世界杯赛场的成长轨迹令人动容。波波维奇的战术以务实的4-3-3为基础，强调边路速度和身体对抗。澳大利亚在2022年曾闯入16强并险些淘汰阿根廷，本届目标同样是突破小组赛。

Australia makes its 6th consecutive World Cup appearance, but this is their youngest and least experienced squad in two decades — 17 debutants. Captain Ryan is the second-most-capped goalkeeper in team history (104 caps), while 35-year-old Leckie plays in his 4th World Cup. At 20, Nestory Irankunda has one of the most compelling stories in the tournament: Tanzania refugee camp to the World Cup stage. Popovic's pragmatic 4-3-3 relies on wide speed and physicality. The Socceroos reached R16 in 2022 and nearly knocked out eventual champions Argentina — the group-stage breakthrough is again the target.

### 双重国籍 / Dual Nationals
土耳其队是欧洲"归化"政策最成功的受益者之一，本届阵容有 **5 名德国出生球员**：队长恰尔汗奥卢（生于曼海姆，土耳其裔德国人，曾代表德国U21后转投土耳其）、厄兹詹（科隆出生）、艾汉（盖尔森基兴出生）、耶尔德兹（雷根斯堡出生，父亲土耳其人母亲德国人）和乌尊（雷根斯堡出生）。此外，阿克图尔科卢拥有奥地利血统。这支球队是"德国制造"（Made in Germany）与土耳其足球传统的完美融合——德国青训体系培养的技术纪律加上土耳其人的激情与创造力。

Türkiye is arguably the prime beneficiary of the European-born recruitment model, with **5 German-born players** in this squad: captain Calhanoglu (Mannheim-born, German-raised, ex-Germany U21), Ozcan (Cologne), Ayhan (Gelsenkirchen), Yildiz (Regensburg, Turkish father/German mother), and Uzun (Regensburg). Akturkoglu also has Austrian heritage. This is a "Made in Germany" squad fused with Turkish football tradition — the technical discipline of the German academy system combined with Turkish passion and creativity.

### 历史看点 / Historical Note
土耳其时隔24年重返世界杯！上一次是2002年韩日世界杯，那支由哈坎·苏克、巴斯图尔克和鲁斯图领衔的球队历史性夺得季军——至今仍是土耳其足球的巅峰记忆。此后24年的漫长等待中，土耳其经历了无数次的"差一点"：附加赛功亏一篑、小组赛最后一轮出局。如今，蒙特拉教练打造的4-2-3-1体系以恰尔汗奥卢为节拍器，居莱尔和耶尔德兹组成欧洲最令人兴奋的年轻攻击线之一。皇马新星居莱尔已是世界级天赋的代名词，而20岁的耶尔德兹被誉为"土耳其内马尔"。2002年的季军是目标还是包袱？土耳其在D组首战即迎来硬仗。

Türkiye is back at the World Cup after 24 years! Their last appearance was the legendary 2002 campaign — that Hakan Sukur, Basturk, and Rustu-led side stormed to a third-place finish, still the pinnacle of Turkish football. The 24-year wait has been agonizing: playoff heartbreaks, final-matchday eliminations. Now, Montella's 4-2-3-1 is orchestrated by Calhanoglu as the metronome, with Guler and Yildiz forming one of Europe's most exciting young attacking duos. Real Madrid's Guler is already synonymous with world-class talent, while 20-year-old Yildiz has been dubbed the "Turkish Neymar." Is the 2002 bronze medal a target or a burden? Group D delivers a heavyweight opener.
