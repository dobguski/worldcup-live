### 双重国籍 / Dual Nationals
荷兰队与移民后裔球员的关系是球队DNA的核心部分。范戴克的母亲为苏里南裔，加克波的父亲为多哥人，克鲁伊维特（传奇前锋帕特里克之子）的母亲为库拉索裔，蒂尔出生于赞比亚，赖因德斯拥有印尼血统。但本届最大的遗憾是哈维·西蒙斯因ACL重伤缺阵——这位苏里南裔天才球员原本被寄予厚望。昆滕·廷贝尔与尤里恩·廷贝尔是双胞胎兄弟，分别在阿森纳和马赛效力，成为世界杯罕见的双胞胎同队佳话。15名英超球员使荷兰队成为本届赛事"英格兰化"程度最高的非英球队。

The Oranje's relationship with immigrant-origin players is central to the team's DNA. Van Dijk has a Surinamese mother; Gakpo's father is Togolese; Kluivert (son of legendary striker Patrick) has Curaçaoan heritage on his mother's side; Til was born in Zambia; Reijnders has Indonesian ancestry. The biggest blow is Xavi Simons missing the tournament with an ACL injury — the Surinamese-descended prodigy was expected to shine. Quinten and Jurrien Timber are twins (Marseille and Arsenal respectively), one of the rare twin-pair stories at a World Cup. With 15 Premier League players, the Netherlands is the most "Anglicized" non-English squad at the tournament.

### 历史看点 / Historical Note
荷兰队以三次世界杯亚军的身份来到2026——"无冕之王"的标签既是荣耀也是枷锁。1974年克鲁伊夫的全攻全守、1978年伦森布林克的遗憾、2010年伊涅斯塔第116分钟的致命一击——三代荷兰足球人的世界杯梦想均以心碎告终。科曼执教下的荷兰采用务实的4-3-3/4-2-3-1体系，范戴克领衔的防线加上德容的中场调度、加克波的冲击力和德佩的经验，构成了一条从后到前的完整中轴。15名英超球员意味着这支球队对高强度节奏非常适应。哈维·西蒙斯的缺席削弱了创造力，但昆滕·廷贝尔的崛起提供了新的可能性。F组云集荷兰、日本、瑞典三大劲旅，小组头名争夺将异常激烈。

The Netherlands arrives at their 12th World Cup carrying three runner-up finishes — "the best team never to win it" is both badge of honor and psychological burden. Cruyff's Total Football in 1974, Rensenbrink's post in 1978, Iniesta's 116th-minute dagger in 2010 — three generations of Dutch football dreams ending in heartbreak. Koeman's pragmatic 4-3-3/4-2-3-1 features Van Dijk's defensive command, De Jong's midfield orchestration, Gakpo's attacking thrust, and Depay's experience — a complete spine. With 15 Premier League players, this squad is built for high-tempo intensity. Xavi Simons' ACL absence diminishes the creative spark, but Quinten Timber's emergence offers new possibilities. Group F packs Netherlands, Japan, and Sweden — the fight for top spot will be ferocious.

### 双重国籍 / Dual Nationals
日本队历史上以本土球员为主，但本届阵容的国际化程度创下新高。最引人注目的是门将锡安·铃木（Zion Suzuki）——出生于美国阿肯色州，父亲为加纳裔美国人，母亲为日本人。他同时拥有代表美国、加纳和日本三个国家的资格，最终选择为日本出战。他的体格（190cm）和爆发力使其成为日本队史最具运动天赋的门将。此外，久保建英4岁赴西班牙，先后在巴萨和皇马青训营接受培养，被视为"拉玛西亚制造的日本武士"。小久保玲央出生于日本但在葡萄牙足球体系中成长。总体而言，23名球员在欧洲联赛效力，创造了日本队史之最。

Japan has traditionally been a domestically-developed squad, but this edition's internationalization reaches new heights. The standout is goalkeeper Zion Suzuki — born in Arkansas, USA, to a Ghanaian-American father and Japanese mother. He was eligible for the USA, Ghana, and Japan, ultimately choosing the Samurai Blue. His 190cm frame and explosiveness make him the most athletic goalkeeper in Japanese history. Meanwhile, Takefusa Kubo moved to Spain at age 4, trained in both Barcelona and Real Madrid academies — a "La Masia-made Samurai." Leo Kokubo was Japan-born but developed entirely in Portugal's football system. Overall, 23 of 26 players compete in European leagues — a Japanese record.

### 历史看点 / Historical Note
日本足球已走到十字路口：四次闯入16强却始终无法突破那道天花板。2022年卡塔尔世界杯上，日本队连胜德国和西班牙震惊世界，却在16强点球大战中不敌克罗地亚——那个下午的泪水和悔恨至今仍是日本足球集体记忆中的痛。森保一继续执掌教鞭，战术体系从2022年的防守反击逐步向控球进攻进化。然而，三笘薰（膝伤）、南野拓实和守田英正的伤缺使得攻击端创造力大打折扣。久保建英和堂安律必须承担更多的进攻发起职责。长友佑都以39岁高龄成为首位参加五届世界杯的亚洲球员——这一里程碑式的成就超越了足球本身的意义。F组与荷兰和瑞典同组，蓝武士的16强突破之路荆棘密布。

Japanese football stands at a crossroads: four Round of 16 appearances, four failures to break through. In Qatar 2022, Japan stunned the world by beating Germany and Spain in the group stage, only to fall to Croatia on penalties in the Round of 16 — the tears and regrets of that afternoon remain etched in Japan's collective football memory. Moriyasu stays at the helm, evolving the tactical approach from 2022's counter-attacking pragmatism toward more possession-based attacking football. But injuries to Kaoru Mitoma (knee), Takumi Minamino, and Hidemasa Morita severely deplete the creative arsenal. Kubo and Doan must shoulder greater playmaking responsibility. At 39, Yuto Nagatomo becomes the first Asian player to appear in five World Cups — a milestone that transcends football. Drawn with Netherlands and Sweden in Group F, the Samurai Blue's path to a historic quarterfinal is treacherous.

### 双重国籍 / Dual Nationals
瑞典队的多元背景是北欧移民社会的缩影。伊萨克的父母为厄立特里亚难民，他本人在斯德哥尔摩郊区成长，已成为世界级前锋；哲凯赖什的父母为匈牙利移民；埃兰加的父亲为喀麦隆前国脚约瑟夫·埃兰加；阿亚里的父母为突尼斯移民；泽内利拥有科索沃阿尔巴尼亚血统。最引人注目的是主教练格雷厄姆·波特——一位英格兰人执掌瑞典国家队，在现代足球中极为罕见。波特以战术多变和球员发展能力著称，他的4-2-3-1体系旨在最大化伊萨克-哲凯赖什-埃兰加这条令人生畏的锋线。可惜德扬·库卢塞夫斯基因伤缺阵，否则瑞典的攻击力将达到恐怖级别。

Sweden's diverse squad mirrors Nordic immigration society. Isak's parents are Eritrean refugees — he grew up in Stockholm's suburbs and has become a world-class forward; Gyokeres' parents are Hungarian immigrants; Elanga's father is former Cameroon international Joseph Elanga; Ayari's parents are Tunisian immigrants; Zeneli has Kosovar Albanian heritage. Most remarkably, manager Graham Potter — an Englishman leading the Swedish national team, a rarity in modern football. Known for tactical versatility and player development, Potter's 4-2-3-1 is designed to maximize the Isak-Gyokeres-Elanga trident. Dejan Kulusevski's injury absence is a major blow — with him, Sweden's attack would be genuinely terrifying.

The spine is anchored by Lindelof and Hien, supported by Tottenham supernova Bergvall. Sweden displayed outstanding tactical cohesion in qualifying. The Isak-Gyokeres partnership may be the most destructive dual-striker combination outside the traditional superpowers at this World Cup.

### 双重国籍 / Dual Nationals
突尼斯队是非洲"归化"政策最活跃的执行者之一，本届阵容拥有令人瞩目的双重国籍深度。拉尼·赫迪拉——是的，萨米·赫迪拉的亲弟弟——出生于德国斯图加特，曾代表德国青年队，在2026年3月正式完成转籍手续，成为本届赛事最具话题性的球员之一。汉尼拔·梅布里（法国出生，前曼联青训）和伊斯梅尔·加尔比（法国出生，前巴黎圣日耳曼青训）均为法国足球体系培养。扬·瓦莱里出生于法国，埃利亚斯·萨阿德出生于德国。最有趣的是19岁的拉扬·埃卢米——出生于加拿大、效力于温哥华白帽，他选择代表突尼斯被加拿大媒体视为一大流失。然而，队史出场纪录保持者姆萨克尼（104场）落选大名单，拉穆奇教练的这个决定引发了突尼斯国内的激烈讨论。

Tunisia is among Africa's most proactive recruiters of diaspora talent, and this squad features striking dual-national depth. Rani Khedira — yes, Sami Khedira's younger brother — was born in Stuttgart, Germany, represented Germany at youth level, and completed his switch to Tunisia in March 2026, making him one of the most talked-about players at this tournament. Hannibal Mejbri (French-born, ex-Man United academy) and Ismael Gharbi (French-born, ex-PSG academy) are both products of the French system. Yan Valery was born in France; Elias Saad in Germany. Most intriguing is 19-year-old Rayan Elloumi — Canadian-born, Vancouver Whitecaps player — whose choice of Tunisia was mourned by Canadian media. However, record cap-holder Msakni (104 caps) was left out, a decision by coach Lamouchi that has sparked fierce debate in Tunisia.

### 历史看点 / Historical Note
突尼斯是现代世界杯历史中充满悲壮色彩的常客——7次参赛，7次止步小组赛。但他们在预选赛中创造了奇迹：10场比赛零失球，防守纪律令整个非洲区对手绝望。1978年，突尼斯以3-1击败墨西哥，成为第一支在世界杯上获胜的非洲和阿拉伯球队；2022年，他们以1-0击败卫冕冠军法国，再次证明自己有能力制造冷门。拉穆奇（法国出生，曾代表法国国家队）执教的突尼斯以斯希里-赫迪拉双后腰为核心，打造了一条极致务实的防守体系。拉尼·赫迪拉的加盟不仅增添了德甲经验，更带来了世界杯冠军的血统传承（哥哥萨米2014年为德国夺冠）。F组的荷兰、日本和瑞典均为攻势足球的代表，突尼斯的铁桶阵能否再次制造惊喜？阿亚里的横空出世（巴黎圣日耳曼青训出身）为进攻端注入了难得的创造力。

Tunisia is one of the World Cup's tragic regulars — 7 appearances, 7 group-stage exits. Yet they produced a miracle in qualifying: 10 matches, zero goals conceded, a defensive record that suffocated every African opponent. In 1978, Tunisia beat Mexico 3-1 to become the first African and Arab team ever to win a World Cup match. In 2022, they beat defending champions France 1-0 — proving again their giant-killing credentials. Lamouchi (French-born, former France international) has built Tunisia around the Skhiri-Khedira double pivot, constructing an ultra-pragmatic defensive system. Rani Khedira's addition brings not just Bundesliga experience but World Cup-winning bloodline (brother Sami won it with Germany in 2014). Group F features Netherlands, Japan, and Sweden — all attack-minded sides. Can Tunisia's defensive fortress spring another surprise? The emergence of Khalil Ayari (PSG academy) injects rare creativity into the attacking third.
