### 历史看点 / Historical Note
阿尔及利亚时隔12年重返世界杯舞台。最大看点是卢卡·齐达内——传奇齐达内之子——通过祖母卡比利亚血统于2025年9月成功转换国籍，并因下颌骨折佩戴保护面罩出场。此外，多位法国出生的球员构成了球队核心：阿瓦尔（前法国国脚）、古伊里（72次法国青年队出场）、艾特-努里（前法国U21）均选择为阿尔及利亚效力。值得注意的落选者包括本纳赛尔、布内贾和队史最佳射手斯利马尼（43球）。

Algeria returns to the World Cup after a 12-year absence. The headline story is Luca Zidane — son of the legendary Zinedine — who switched nationality in September 2025 via his paternal Kabylia grandparents and now wears a protective mask following a broken jaw. The squad leans heavily on French-born talent: Aouar (ex-France senior), Gouiri (72 France youth caps), and Ait-Nouri (ex-France U21) all opted for Algeria. Notable omissions: Bennacer, Bounedjah, and all-time top scorer Slimani (43 goals).
