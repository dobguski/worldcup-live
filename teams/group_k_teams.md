这极有可能是C罗的世界杯谢幕之战。41岁的罗纳尔多第6次踏上世界杯赛场，追平历史纪录。个人荣誉满身——220+场出场、140+球均为男子国际足球历史之最——唯独世界杯冠军是他荣誉室中缺失的最后一件珍品。费利克斯和C罗在俱乐部层面已是利雅得胜利的队友，世界杯赛场再度携手。内维斯（21岁）是本届最受瞩目的新星之一。葡萄牙抽到相对有利的小组，但淘汰赛之路仍需跨越强敌。这或许是"黄金一代"最后的夺冠窗口——也可能是C罗最后的起舞。

This is almost certainly Ronaldo's World Cup farewell. At 41, he steps onto the world stage for a 6th time, tying the all-time record. Every individual record belongs to him — 220+ caps, 140+ goals, both men's all-time marks — yet the World Cup trophy remains the one missing jewel in his crown. Felix joins Ronaldo as club teammates at Al Nassr in a fascinating subplot. 21-year-old Joao Neves is among the tournament's most exciting wonderkids. Portugal enjoys a favorable group draw but faces a gauntlet in the knockouts. This may be the Golden Generation's last window — and Ronaldo's last dance.

### 历史看点 / Historical Note
刚果民主共和国时隔52年重返世界杯——上次参赛还是1974年以"扎伊尔"之名。本次之旅最震撼的数据：全部26名球员均在海外联赛效力，无一人在刚果国内踢球。防线迎来重大归化胜利：万-比萨卡（前英格兰U20/U21，曾被成年队征召）和图安泽贝（前英格兰U19-U21）均选择为祖先的土地而战。德萨布雷和前队长扎夸尼亲自登门拜访万-比萨卡父母的故事已成为刚果足球传奇。卡库塔（前法国所有青年级别、2010年U19欧锦赛最佳球员）带来丰富的顶级赛事经验。

DR Congo returns to the World Cup after 52 years — last here in 1974 as Zaire. The staggering stat: all 26 players are based outside DR Congo, a squad drawn entirely from European and MENA leagues. The defense features major recruitment coups: Wan-Bissaka (ex-England U20/U21, once called to England seniors) and Tuanzebe (ex-England U19-U21) both chose to represent the land of their ancestors. The story of Desabre and former captain Zakuani personally visiting Wan-Bissaka's parents at home has entered Congolese football folklore. Kakuta (ex-France all youth levels, 2010 U19 Euro Player of the Tournament) brings elite pedigree.
