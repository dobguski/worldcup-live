### 双重国籍 / Dual Nationals
拉波尔特出生于法国，2021年转籍西班牙。亚马尔父亲为摩洛哥裔、母亲为赤道几内亚裔，他选择代表西班牙。尼科·威廉姆斯的父母是加纳难民，其兄伊纳基选择代表加纳征战。多元化的血统构成了这支西班牙队的独特图景。

Aymeric Laporte was French-born and switched in 2021. Lamine Yamal has a Moroccan father and Equatoguinean mother, yet chose Spain. Nico Williams' Ghanaian parents arrived as refugees in Spain; his brother Inaki plays for Ghana. A diverse tapestry of backgrounds shapes this Spanish squad.

For the first time ever, Spain arrives at a World Cup with ZERO Real Madrid players. Barcelona contributes 8 while Real Madrid supplies none — a seismic shift that has sparked intense debate. De la Fuente's side are reigning European champions on a 33-match unbeaten run. Morata has retired from international duty. Without a classic No.9, Spain relies on possession and explosive wingers led by 18-year-old phenomenon Lamine Yamal. Can this Barcelona-heavy squad replicate 2010?

### 双重国籍 / Dual Nationals
佛得角是经典的散居型国家队。洛根·科斯塔出生于法国，是队内唯一效力五大联赛的球员。守门员CJ·多斯桑托斯出生于美国费城，曾代表美国青年队出战，后转籍佛得角。后卫皮科/罗伯托·洛佩斯出生于爱尔兰，效力于爱尔兰联赛沙姆洛克流浪者——他是历史上首位参加世界杯的爱尔兰联赛球员。蒙泰罗和杜阿尔特兄弟出生于荷兰鹿特丹。球员遍布15+联赛，遍及欧洲、亚洲和美洲。

Cape Verde is the quintessential diaspora squad. Logan Costa is French-born and the only top-5 league player. GK CJ dos Santos was Philadelphia-born and switched from USA. Pico/Roberto Lopes is Irish-born — the first-ever League of Ireland player at a World Cup! Monteiro and the Duarte brothers were born in Rotterdam, Netherlands. Players are spread across 15+ leagues spanning Europe, Asia, and the Americas.

Cape Verde, a West African island nation of just ~600,000 people, makes its World Cup debut. The squad spans 15+ leagues — from MLS to the Russian Premier League, Cyprus to Finland — the quintessential diaspora nation. In qualifying, they topped a group containing Cameroon and Angola. 36-year-old captain Ryan Mendes leads this team at the twilight of his career. Can the Blue Sharks shock the world on their first dance?

### 历史看点 / Historical Note
这是后苏亚雷斯-卡瓦尼时代的首届世界杯! 苏亚雷斯(队史最佳射手69球,已退役)曾请求复出但被贝尔萨婉拒。穆斯莱拉同样复出，创纪录第五次参加世界杯。贝尔萨的高压逼抢风格给球队注入新灵魂。巴尔韦德是皇马中场的绝对核心，努涅斯转战沙特后状态成疑。两届世界冠军乌拉圭，在狂人教练的带领下，会是本届的潜在黑马吗？

This is Uruguay's FIRST World Cup without Suarez AND Cavani! Suarez (all-time top scorer with 69 goals, now retired) offered to return but was excluded by Bielsa. Muslera came out of retirement for a record 5th World Cup. Bielsa's high-pressing, high-intensity style has transformed the team. Valverde is the midfield engine, while Nunez's form after moving to Saudi Arabia is a question mark. Two-time champions Uruguay, under the mad genius Bielsa — potential dark horses?
