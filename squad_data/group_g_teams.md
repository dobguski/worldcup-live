This is the Golden Generation's 4th and almost certainly final World Cup together. After the shock group-stage exit in 2022, Belgium seeks redemption. De Bruyne now at Napoli adds a new dimension. Missing Openda and Batshuayi but the KDB+Lukaku spine endures. Can they finally deliver the trophy that eluded them?
